About

Working across media

Alex has nearly two decades of experience adapting complex information into compelling formats for broad audiences. At Uptime, he led a team producing interactive summaries of books and documentaries. At TED-Ed, he researched, scripted, and edited hundreds of lessons spanning philosophy, history, and science.

His work has appeared in American Affairs, The Point, and Voice of America.
